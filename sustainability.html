Sustainability

Responsibility in Every Stitch

At Firenze Knitwear, sustainability is not a label — it's a way of working.

From the ground up in Madagascar, we’re committed to protecting our environment, empowering our people, and contributing positively to our community and the global fashion ecosystem.

Caring for the Environment

We believe responsible production starts with small, consistent choices.

  • Reduce material waste through efficient cutting and knitting methods
  • Reuse and recycle leftover yarn and off-cuts where possible
  • Prioritize low-impact yarns such as organic cotton and recycled blends
  • Explore local sourcing and packaging to reduce our carbon footprint

Empowering People

Our team is the heart of Firenze Knitwear.

  • Equal opportunity hiring (50% women in our workforce)
  • Ongoing technical training and development
  • Safe, respectful, and inclusive working conditions
  • Internal growth and long-term employment

Giving Back to Our Community

We are rooted in Madagascar, and we take that responsibility seriously.

  • Donating excess garments and materials to local families and charities
  • Supporting school programs and training workshops
  • Encouraging small-scale outreach efforts with local partners

Our Sustainability Mindset

Sustainability for us is not about perfection — it’s about purpose.

Every thread, every team member, every garment we ship reflects our promise:

To make knitwear with care — for people, planet, and progress.
