Responsibility in Every Stitch

At Firenze Knitwear, sustainability is knitted into everything we do — from reducing waste and using better materials to investing in people and giving back to our community.

  • Minimize waste and reuse yarns where possible
  • Prioritize lower-impact yarns and local sourcing
  • Empower a workforce that’s 50% women
  • Support education, training, and local outreach

We don’t strive for perfection — we work with purpose.
Every garment we make reflects our care for the environment, our people, and a better future for fashion.
