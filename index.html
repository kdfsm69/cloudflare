About Us

Industrial Knitwear Made with Precision in Madagascar

At Firenze Knitwear, we believe every thread tells a story. Born from a union of Italian design elegance and Madagascar’s rich textile heritage, our company is built on the values of precision, sustainability, and cultural integrity.

Designed for the World, Made in Madagascar

Founded in the heart of Madagascar, our factory combines advanced knitting technology with a streamlined, quality-focused production process. The name “Firenze” pays tribute to the timeless beauty and creativity of Florence, Italy—our design muse. Every garment we produce reflects that spirit, reinterpreted for the global market.
