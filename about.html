Industrial Knitwear Made with Precision in Madagascar

At Firenze Knitwear, we believe every thread tells a story. Born from a union of Italian design elegance and Madagascar’s rich textile heritage, our company is built on the values of precision, sustainability, and cultural integrity.

Designed for the World, Made in Madagascar

Founded in the heart of Madagascar, our factory combines advanced knitting technology with a streamlined, quality-focused production process. The name “Firenze” pays tribute to the timeless beauty and creativity of Florence, Italy—our design muse. Every garment we produce reflects that spirit, reinterpreted for the global market.

What Defines Us

  • Full-Cycle Production – We manage everything in-house: sampling, knitting, finishing, and final packaging — ensuring consistency, lead time control, and tailored solutions.
  • Knitwear Production – From lightweight sweaters to warm winter pullovers, we manufacture a diverse range of knitted garments tailored to client specifications.
  • Knit Accessories – Scarves, beanies, and other custom accessories that complement and expand our clients’ collections.
  • Global Reach – We proudly supply renowned fashion labels and department stores, with duty-free access to key markets including Europe, the USA, Canada, Japan, and South Africa.
  • Versatile Capabilities – Our machines support multiple gauges, yarn types, and textures, offering high flexibility for varied client needs.
  • Inclusive Team Culture – Our workforce is 50% women, reflecting our commitment to equal opportunity and local empowerment.
  • Process-Driven – From yarn to packed garment, every stage is engineered for precision, quality, lead time optimization, and client satisfaction.
  • Sustainability – We are committed to responsible sourcing, ethical production practices, and long-term environmental care.
  • Innovation – We integrate industrial engineering with textile creativity to develop efficient, high-quality production flows—from SMV optimization to 3D-printed accessories.

Ready to Bring Your Vision to Life

Our knitwear reaches clients around the globe, but its roots are deeply grounded in the culture, resilience, and beauty of Madagascar. Whether you're a fashion label looking for trusted production partners or a brand exploring sustainable textile solutions, Firenze Knitwear is ready to bring your vision to life.
